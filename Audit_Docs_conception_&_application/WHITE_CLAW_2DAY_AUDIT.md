## PART 3: WHITE CLAW — Done-For-You Solution Design

### Positioning

**White Claw** = *"AI Agent Setup. Done. No Debugging."*

One price. One outcome. You get a working AI setup without touching configs.

---

### Offer Structure

| Tier | Price | First 25 | After 25 | What They Get |
|------|-------|----------|----------|---------------|
| **White Claw Lite** | $49 | ✅ | $100 | 1 integration, 1 custom automation, 7-day support |
| **White Claw Pro** | $99 | $149 | $199 | 3 integrations, 2 automations, 30-day support |

### Lite ($49 → $100) — Validation Tier

**Target:** First 25 = proof of concept, testimonials, case studies

**Deliverables:**
- Hardware-agnostic OpenClaw config (Gemini-first, no local dependency)
- 1 integration of choice: Notion OR Slack OR Gmail
- 1 custom skill/automation to spec
- 7-day email support
- 15-min kickoff call + Loom walkthrough

**Time to deliver:** 24–48 hours

**Why $49 first 25:**
- Low friction for validation
- Fast "yes" from hot leads
- Easy to say "I'm one of the first 25"
- Creates scarcity + FOMO for $100

---

### Pro ($99 → $199) — Scale Tier

**Deliverables:**
- Everything in Lite
- 3 integrations (Notion + Slack + one more)
- 2 custom skills
- 30-day support (24hr response SLA)
- 30-min training call

**Time to deliver:** 3–5 days

## PART 4: MICRO-SAAS + SERVICE DUAL MODEL

### As a Service (Human-Delivered)

- You (or a VA) do the setup
- Use existing WhiteClaw playbook, intake forms, 14-day checklist
- Condense to 24–48 hrs for Lite, 3–5 days for Pro

### As Micro-SaaS (Automated)

- **Lite tier:** Template configs + guided wizard that generates `openclaw.json` + 1 skill from a form
- **Pro tier:** Same wizard + Notion/Slack OAuth flows that auto-configure
- Stripe/Gumroad for payment
- Automated email sequence: intake → config delivery → support

**Hybrid model (recommended for launch):**
- Service first: Manual delivery for first 25
- Capture process, document steps
- Productize: Turn into wizard/template product for scale
